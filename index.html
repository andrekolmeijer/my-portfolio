about me

Hi, I am passionate about the web.

Ever since I was growing up, I had this awe for the world wide web. It was this vast thing that I didn't understand and just had to know about. But how? I wasn't into computers or the web... my bachelor was in chemistry!

And then I met a friend. A friend who sat down with me and told me all about it. Showed me. Explained to me. Helped me understand. The door was open, never to be closed again.

It took me years to build my know-how and my confidence. But here you are, on my website. Let's code something together!
